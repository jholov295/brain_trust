Module Chronos.TestCaseRecorder

[Internal] TestCaseRecorder is responsible for recording events of a Chronos.TestCase's execution to an instance of Chronos.TestCaseResults. Actions and failures bubble up from Chronos.TestCaseRecorder, to inform the status of Chronos.TestSuiteRecorders and Chronos.TestRunRecorders.
