Module Chronos.TestSuiteRecorder

[Internal] TestSuiteRecorder is responsible for recording events of a Chronos.TestSuite's execution to an instance of Chronos.TestSuiteResults. Actions and failures bubble up from Chronos.TestSuiteRecorder, to inform the status of Chronos.TestRunRecorders.
